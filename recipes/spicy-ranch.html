Spicy Ranch

Quick and Easy Spicy Ranch

This is my families go-to Ranch for any Salad or Dipping occasions. Using Hidden Valley Ranch seasoning and Texas Pete HotSauce with some added seasoning creates the perfect bit of spice. We generally will make it a few hours before needed so it can sit in the fridge and get a bit thicker. Will also taste spicier after it has thickened up more.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack Ranch Dressing Mix
  • 1 cup Mayo
  • 1 cup Sour Cream
  • 1/8 tsp Paprika
  • 1/8 tsp Cayenne Pepper
  • 1/8 tsp Chili Powder
  • 1 tbsp Texas Pete HotSauce
