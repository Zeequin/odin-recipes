Spicy Ranch

Quick and Easy Spicy Ranch

This is my families go-to Ranch for any Salad or Dipping occasions. Using Hidden Valley Ranch seasoning and Texas Pete HotSauce with some added seasoning creates the perfect bit of spice. We generally will make it a few hours before needed so it can sit in the fridge and get a bit thicker. Will also taste spicier after it has thickened up more.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack Ranch Dressing Mix
  • 1 cup Mayo
  • 1 cup Sour Cream
  • 1/8 tsp Paprika
  • 1/8 tsp Cayenne Pepper
  • 1/8 tsp Chili Powder
  • 1 tbsp Texas Pete HotSauce

Directions

  1. Mix mayo and sour cream in container until one solid color.
  2. Add paprika, chili powder, and cayenne pepper into mix. Stir until orange/red color.
  3. Add HotSauce and mix well. Adjust spice level if needed.
  4. Leave in fridge until ready to use. Store up to 2 weeks in container.

Notes

  • If to thick add water and mix until desired texture.
  • If to spicy add equal parts mayo and sour cream.
