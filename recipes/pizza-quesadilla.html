Pizza Quesadilla

Super Simple Pizza Quesadilla

This super simple Pizza Quesadilla is a stable in my house. We will toss these together wether its for breakfast, lunch, or just need something to munch on. Only takes 5 minutes to make. If you're like me and like to dip pizza in ranch, check out this Spicy Ranch recipe.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Shredded Mozzarella
  • Pepperonis
  • Mushrooms

Directions

  1. Lay Tortilla flat and spread sauce across
  2. On one half of the Tortilla add Cheese
  3. Add desired amount of Pepperonis and Mushrooms on top of the Cheese
  4. Top off with another layer of cheese then fold Tortilla closed
  5. Bring skillet to Medium heat and toast each side of Tortilla evenly until cheese has melted
